The Best Movies According to Jonathan

My top 3 movies off all-time.

Expendables

I really like this film, because it is a war genre and the actors are very cool when performing their roles.

Parasite

This film tells the story of a family who live as they are, who then enter one by one and work in a wealthy family, creating a very dramatic chaotic atmosphere.

Naruto

Since I was in elementary school, I really like watching Naruto anime films, because there are many things that can be taken from the film, starting from friendship, sacrifice, struggle and many more.
